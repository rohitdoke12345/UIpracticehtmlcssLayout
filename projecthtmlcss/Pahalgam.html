Pahalgam

Betaab Valley

Betaab Valley is a picturesque valley in Pahalgam, named after the Bollywood movie "Betaab" which was shot here.

1. Location: Betaab Valley is located around 15 kilometers from Pahalgam town in Kashmir, India.

2. Scenic Beauty: Surrounded by lush greenery, snow-capped mountains, and the Lidder River, Betaab Valley offers stunning natural beauty and a tranquil ambiance.

3. Activities: Visitors can enjoy various activities such as picnicking, horse riding, and trekking in Betaab Valley, amidst its serene surroundings.

4. Film Shooting: The valley gained popularity after being featured in the Bollywood film "Betaab", starring Sunny Deol and Amrita Singh, which contributed to its name.

5. Photography: Betaab Valley is a paradise for photographers, offering breathtaking views and opportunities to capture the beauty of nature in its pristine form.

Aru Valley

Aru Valley is a charming valley in Pahalgam, known for its lush meadows, gushing streams, and panoramic views of the Himalayas.

1. Location: Aru Valley is situated approximately 12 kilometers from Pahalgam town in Kashmir, India.

2. Natural Beauty: Surrounded by dense forests, snow-capped peaks, and alpine meadows, Aru Valley offers a pristine and unspoiled environment for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

3. Adventure Activities: The valley is a hub for adventure activities such as trekking, camping, and trout fishing, attracting adventure seekers from around the world.

4. Wildlife: Aru Valley is home to a variety of wildlife species, including Himalayan brown bears, musk deer, and snow leopards, making it a paradise for wildlife enthusiasts and photographers.

5. Camping: Adventurous travelers can camp amidst the scenic surroundings of Aru Valley, under the starlit sky, surrounded by the tranquility of nature.

Lidder River

Lidder River is a pristine river flowing through Pahalgam, offering opportunities for trout fishing, river rafting, and leisurely walks along its banks.

1. Location: Lidder River flows through the town of Pahalgam in Kashmir, India.

2. Scenic Beauty: The river is surrounded by lush greenery, pine forests, and snow-capped mountains, offering a picturesque setting for visitors to enjoy.

3. Trout Fishing: Anglers can enjoy trout fishing in the clear waters of the Lidder River, which is known for its abundant population of brown and rainbow trout.

4. River Rafting: Adventure enthusiasts can experience the thrill of white water river rafting on the rapids of the Lidder River, with varying levels of difficulty to suit all experience levels.

5. Riverside Walks: Visitors can take leisurely walks along the banks of the Lidder River, enjoying the serene ambiance and panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes.
