Gulmarg Gondola

Gulmarg Gondola is one of the highest cable cars in the world, offering breathtaking views of the Himalayas and surrounding valleys.

1. Location: Gulmarg Gondola is located in Gulmarg, Kashmir, India.

2. Height: The gondola reaches an altitude of over 13,000 feet, providing stunning panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

3. Two Phases: The gondola operates in two phases, with Phase 1 taking visitors from Gulmarg to Kongdoori Station and Phase 2 ascending further to the Apharwat Peak.

4. Scenic Beauty: The ride offers unparalleled views of snow-capped peaks, lush green meadows, and dense forests, making it a must-do activity in Gulmarg.

5. Adventure Activities: Visitors can indulge in various adventure activities such as skiing, snowboarding, and trekking at the Apharwat Peak, accessible via the gondola.

Gulmarg Golf Course

Gulmarg Golf Course is one of the highest golf courses in the world, set amidst stunning natural beauty and surrounded by snow-capped mountains.

1. Location: Gulmarg Golf Course is situated in the town of Gulmarg, Kashmir, India.

2. Altitude: At an altitude of 8,690 feet, the golf course offers a unique playing experience with challenging terrain and breathtaking views.

3. Design: Designed in 1904 by the British, Gulmarg Golf Course is renowned for its scenic layout, natural hazards, and lush green fairways.

4. Winter Sports: In winter, the golf course transforms into a popular skiing destination, attracting enthusiasts from around the world to its slopes.

5. Spectacular Views: Golfers can enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding Himalayan peaks, including Nanga Parbat and Mount Harmukh, while playing amidst pristine natural surroundings.

Alpather Lake

Alpather Lake is a scenic high-altitude lake in Gulmarg, surrounded by snow-covered mountains and lush green meadows.

1. Location: Alpather Lake is located about 13 kilometers from Gulmarg town in Kashmir, India.

2. Altitude: Situated at an altitude of approximately 11,000 feet, the lake remains frozen for most of the year due to its high elevation.

3. Scenic Beauty: Surrounded by snow-capped peaks and verdant meadows, Alpather Lake offers a tranquil and picturesque setting for visitors to enjoy.

4. Trekking: The lake is a popular trekking destination, with trekking trails leading through beautiful alpine forests and offering stunning views of the surrounding landscapes.

5. Camping: Adventurous travelers can camp near Alpather Lake to experience the serenity of the Himalayas and witness mesmerizing sunrises and sunsets over the snow-covered peaks.
